Hanami in the Park

April 2026 • Community Event

Our Hanami gathering brings members, families, and friends together to enjoy the beauty of spring. Inspired by the Japanese tradition of flower viewing, this event is a moment to relax outdoors, share food, and celebrate friendship in a peaceful atmosphere.

Guests can enjoy a picnic-style afternoon, seasonal Japanese treats, and activities for children. It is also a wonderful opportunity for newcomers to meet members of the Japan Luxembourg Association and discover our community spirit.
